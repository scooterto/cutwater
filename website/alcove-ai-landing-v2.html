FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Alcove AI uses on-device processing and end-to-end encryption. Your unencrypted data never leaves your device. When querying knowledge bases, only vector embeddings are sent—not your actual data. All sensitive processing happens locally through our Mini-Agent.

RAG (Retrieval-Augmented Generation) is our intelligent information retrieval system. It divides your queries into privacy-safe components, queries relevant knowledge bases using vector embeddings, and synthesizes responses while keeping your private data encrypted and on-device.

Private knowledge bases store your encrypted personal data on your device—only you can access it. Public knowledge bases are community-driven repositories that anyone can contribute to and benefit from. You earn tokens for contributing to public datasets.

Tokens are earned by contributing to public knowledge bases through adding data, making edits, and voting on quality. You can use these tokens to subscribe to premium datasets in the Data Marketplace, expanding your available information sources.

Edge hosting means data processing happens on your local device rather than remote servers. This provides faster response times, eliminates data transmission risks, and ensures your sensitive information is never exposed to external networks or third parties.
