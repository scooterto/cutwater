Design Philosophy

The Cutwater logo represents our core mission: navigating the complex waters of DeFi with precision and stability. Each element carries intentional meaning.

Water Droplet

Represents liquidity and fluid movement in decentralized finance. The smooth curves convey stability and continuous flow.

Diagonal Slashes

"Cutting through water" symbolizes efficient market navigation and the ability to find opportunity in any condition.

Dual Lines

The parallel slashes represent our delta-neutral approach: long spot positions balanced by short perpetuals.

Geometric Precision

Clean lines and mathematical construction convey technical credibility, accuracy, and professional rigor.
