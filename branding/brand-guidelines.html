01 — Logo

Logo — Split Balance

The Cutwater "Split Balance" logo features three key elements: a C-curve representing our brand initial, an animated balance line symbolizing equilibrium, and a center dot representing stability. Together they convey the delta-neutral strategy of balancing long and short positions.
