Brand Overview

Alcove AI is a privacy-first artificial intelligence platform that keeps user data secure through on-device processing and encrypted knowledge bases. The brand conveys trust, innovation, and security while maintaining a modern, approachable aesthetic.

Brand Attributes

  • ✦ Trustworthy - Privacy and security are core values
  • ✦ Innovative - Cutting-edge AI technology
  • ✦ Transparent - Clear about how data is handled
  • ✦ Empowering - Gives users control over their data
